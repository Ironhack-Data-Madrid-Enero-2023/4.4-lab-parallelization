                    <h1 class="page-title">\n                  About Data Science              </h1>                              <div class="region region-content">\n  <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system">\n\n      \n  <div class="content">\n                        <span property="dc:title" content="About Data Science" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span><span property="sioc:num_replies" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span>  \n      \n    \n  <div class="content">\n    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>"Data Science" means the scientific study of the creation, validation and transformation of data to create meaning.  </p>\n<p>A "Data Scientist" is a professional who uses scientific methods to liberate and create meaning from raw data - somebody who can play with data, spot trends and learn truths few others know. Data scientists are inquisitive: exploring, asking questions, doing \xe2\x80\x9cwhat if\xe2\x80\x9d analysis, questioning existing assumptions and processes.</p>\n<p>The data scientist has a solid foundation in machine learning, algorithms, modeling, statistics, analytics, math and strong business acumen, coupled with the ability to communicate findings to both business and IT leaders in a way that can influence how an organization approaches a business challenge.</p>\n<p>Data science is different from traditional business analytics - which is defined as the practice of iterative, methodical exploration of an organization\xe2\x80\x99s data with emphasis on statistical analysis. </p>\n<p>Data science uses scientific principles to get meaning from data and uses machine learning and algorithms to manage and extract actionable, valuable intelligence from large data sets.</p>\n</div></div></div>  </div>\n        <footer>\n          </footer>\n        </div>\n  \n</div> <!--
